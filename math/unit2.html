When faced with a number like 1024, you can simplify it by turning 1024 into 2^10.
This can also be solved by using logarithms.
When using logarithms, use the base and put it behind the log, put the said big number in the parentheses, then put the exponent on the right side of the equal sign.

Ex: log2(1024)=10
